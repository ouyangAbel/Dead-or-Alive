GAME MECHANICS

CORE SYSTEMS

01

STRIKE

Strikes are the most fundamental offensive tool. Combine punches (P) and kicks (K) with directional inputs for a wide variety of moves. Strikes beat Throws.

INPUT: P / K / 6P / 3K / 236P
02

THROW

Throws catch blocking opponents. Press T (Throw) at close range to grab. Throws beat Holds but lose to Strikes.

INPUT: T / 6T / 4T / 2T / 46T
03

HOLD

Input a reversal hold just before an attack connects to deflect and counter. Different holds are needed for high, mid, and low attacks.

INPUT: 4H (High) / 1H (Mid) / 2H (Low)
04

CRITICAL BURST

Dealing enough damage sends opponents into Critical State. During this window, a Critical Burst attack breaks through their guard for high-damage follow-ups.

INPUT: P+K after Critical State
05

DEADLY RUSH

Input specific Rush Combo sequences to automatically chain into Deadly Rush attacks, simplifying combo execution while maintaining strong damage.

INPUT: Auto-triggers on P / P / K chain
06

SIDESTEP

Press 8 or 2 to sidestep linear attacks. Follow up immediately with a sidestep attack to punish. Essential for dodging predictable rushdown.

INPUT: 8 / 2 (Sidestep) - P / K (Counter)
PRO TIPS

PRO TIPS

TIP #01

Master the Triangle

Strike beats Throw, Throw beats Hold, Hold beats Strike. Don't rely on one option. Study your opponent's habits and choose the right counter.

TIP #02

Use Stage Boundaries

Walls and corners are your best friends. Push opponents near walls for wall combo extensions that dramatically boost damage.

TIP #03

Patience with Critical Burst

Don't rush the CB after triggering Critical State. Wait for your opponent to attempt a counter or block, then strike for maximum impact.

TIP #04

Frame Data Awareness

Know the frame data of your key moves. Use fast jabs when at advantage; respect your opponent's turn when at disadvantage.

TIP #05

Stop Button Mashing

DOA is a mind game, not a speed contest. Every input should be intentional. Stay calm when getting combo'd and look for the gap to escape.

TIP #06

Grind Training Mode

Spend 15 minutes daily in Training Mode. Set the dummy to record common strings and practice holding them. This is the fastest path to ranking up.

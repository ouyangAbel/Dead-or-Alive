TIER EXPLANATION

RANKING CRITERIA

S TIER

Top Tier

Best-in-class tools with few bad matchups. These characters dominate at every level of play and are frequent tournament picks. Strong neutral, combo damage, and defensive options.

A TIER

High Tier

Excellent characters with well-rounded kits. May have one or two slightly unfavorable matchups but remain highly competitive. Solid choices for any tournament run.

B TIER

Mid Tier

Viable characters that require more effort to succeed. They have specific strengths but also notable weaknesses that opponents can exploit. Reward for dedicated mains.

C TIER

Low Tier

Characters with significant matchup struggles. Can still win in the right hands but generally outclassed by higher-tier options. Often requires matchup-specific knowledge to compete.

The Original Dead or Alive 6 Release Date

Dead or Alive 6 launched worldwide on March 1, 2019. Team Ninja developed the game and Koei Tecmo published it simultaneously for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC via Steam. The release came roughly six years after Dead or Alive 5 debuted in 2012, making it one of the most anticipated fighting game sequels of the generation.

The announcement came at E3 2018 during the Sony press conference, where Team Ninja director Yohei Shimbori revealed the game alongside a debut trailer showcasing updated graphics, new mechanics like the Break Gauge, and the return of fan-favorite characters. A playable demo appeared at various fighting game events throughout the second half of 2018, building significant community hype ahead of launch.

At release, Dead or Alive 6 shipped with 26 base roster characters, 20 stages, a story mode, and full online multiplayer. The game received generally positive reviews, with critics praising its accessible combat system, improved visual fidelity over DOA5, and the depth of its Triangle System. The initial reception set the stage for years of post-launch support that would eventually culminate in Last Round.

A Digital Deluxe Edition launched alongside the standard edition, bundling the first season pass with bonus costumes and exclusive items. Early adopters who pre-ordered received bonus character costumes as incentive. The launch window saw strong initial sales, particularly in Japan and across Asian markets where the Dead or Alive franchise has a deeply established competitive community.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Core Strengths

  • very strong throw reward
  • good mix of high and low pressure
  • solid stage carry
  • flashy but practical routes

These strengths define why LISA can compete at a high level.

Main Weaknesses

  • some starters are committal
  • checked by safe mid-heavy fighters
  • needs reads to close rounds cleanly
  • requires layering to beat patient guard

Understanding weaknesses helps you build better practice habits.

Beginner Gameplan

If you are still learning LISA, start with a simple, repeatable gameplan.

  • safe string into throw attempt
  • launcher into basic route
  • poke into reset
  • simple wall ender

Advanced Strategy

At higher levels, LISA becomes more about timing control and layered reads.

  • command throw conditioning
  • delayed low/mid rotations
  • stage-specific damage stacking
  • rhythm changes to bait counters
